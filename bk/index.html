Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Grady Gearbox Gangstaz Robotics Team! We are the award winning robotics team of Grady High School located in Atlanta, Georgia. The Gangstaz are a team of students who participate in a variety of competitions revolving around the design, construction, and operation of robotic technology. The group has been competing successfully since 2004 in the first, VEX/FTC, and best competitions.

The Grady Gearbox Gangstaz are a student driven organization open to any and all students at Grady High School. We use robotics as an oppurtunity to share educational and fun learning experiences at H.W. Grady High School.
